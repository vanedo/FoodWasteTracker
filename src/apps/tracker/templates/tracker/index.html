OUR WORK

1.33 trillion kg's of food is thrown away each year.
We are on a mission to change that.

We believe that helping households reduce food waste will help save our planet.
Along with giving people the information they need to properly store and elongate
the life of their foods, we let them share their food waste decisions with the
wastelogr community so we can track and reduce the amount of food we all waste.
